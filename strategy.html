Our Strategy

A disciplined, systematic approach to generating yield through options premium harvesting in digital asset markets.

Strategy Philosophy

Our investment philosophy is rooted in the belief that systematic, disciplined approaches outperform discretionary trading over time. We focus on harvesting the volatility risk premium inherent in digital asset options markets—a structural inefficiency that has historically provided consistent returns.

The digital asset options market exhibits unique characteristics that create opportunities for skilled volatility traders. Implied volatility consistently trades at a premium to realized volatility, creating a sustainable source of return for options sellers who can manage the associated risks.

  • Systematic approach removes emotional decision-making
  • Focus on repeatable, well-understood risk premiums
  • Quantitative framework for position sizing and timing
  • Continuous refinement based on market evolution

Market Environments

Our strategy is designed to perform across various market conditions, with particular strength in environments characterized by elevated implied volatility and range-bound or gradually trending markets.

While no strategy performs equally well in all market conditions, our approach includes specific adaptations for different volatility regimes:

  • High Volatility: Reduced position sizes with wider strike selection to capture elevated premiums while managing tail risk
  • Normal Volatility: Standard positioning with optimal risk-reward balance for consistent premium collection
  • Low Volatility: Selective opportunities with tighter risk parameters and focus on highest-quality setups
  • Trending Markets: Directional bias incorporated into strike selection while maintaining premium focus

Risk Control Framework

Multiple layers of protection designed to preserve capital and manage drawdowns.

1

Pre-Trade Risk Assessment

Every trade undergoes rigorous analysis including stress testing, scenario analysis, and Greeks evaluation before execution. Position sizes are determined by maximum acceptable loss rather than potential profit.

2

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous portfolio monitoring with automated alerts for risk limit breaches, unusual market conditions, and position-level concerns. 24/7 coverage during active trading periods.

3

Dynamic Hedging

Systematic delta and gamma management with predefined rebalancing triggers. Tail risk protection through strategic use of protective options during elevated risk periods.

4

Drawdown Protocols

Tiered de-risking procedures triggered by predefined portfolio drawdown levels. Systematic reduction in exposure ensures capital preservation during adverse periods.
